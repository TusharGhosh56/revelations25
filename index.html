“Revelations” is an Intra-departmental fest that

is organized by the Department of Computer Science

for the Post graduate students every year. The objective

of this event is to provide a platform for the students

to show case their talents in technical as well as

non-technical events.



The students were divided into multiple groups. In and

around every event, there are about 300+ students competing

against each other. The fest has 10 events in total. The

preliminary rounds for all the events are conducted from

the during the months of March and April.
